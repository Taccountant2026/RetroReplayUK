Warranty

RetroReplay UK warranty policy for refurbished PlayStation 2 consoles.

Warranty Coverage

All consoles sold by RetroReplay UK include a 90-day warranty.

The warranty covers hardware faults related to refurbishment work and normal use.

What the Warranty Covers

  • Disc drive or laser faults
  • Hardware failure related to refurbishment
  • Power or startup issues
  • Faults occurring under normal use

What the Warranty Does Not Cover

  • Damage caused by misuse or accidents
  • Damage caused by third-party modifications
  • Liquid damage
  • Physical damage or cosmetic wear
  • Issues caused by incompatible accessories

Warranty Claims

If you experience a fault during the warranty period, please contact us through the contact page with details of the issue.

You may be asked to provide photos or a description of the problem to help diagnose the issue.

Where a fault is confirmed, we may repair, replace or refund the console depending on the situation.

Shipping for Warranty Claims

Customers may be responsible for shipping the console back for inspection. Return instructions will be provided after a warranty claim has been submitted.
