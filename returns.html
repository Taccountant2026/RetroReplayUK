Returns Policy

Information about returns and refunds for RetroReplay UK products.

Return Eligibility

If your console arrives faulty or develops a fault within the warranty period, please contact us within 30 days of delivery.

We will assess the issue and may offer repair, replacement, or refund depending on the situation.

Faulty Items

Faulty products may be returned for inspection. Once confirmed, we may repair, replace, or refund the item depending on stock availability and the nature of the fault.

Items Not Eligible for Return

  • Damage caused by misuse
  • Damage caused by liquids
  • Products modified by third parties
  • Physical damage after delivery

Return Process

To begin a return request please contact us with your order details and a description of the issue.

Contact Support

Shipping for Returns

Customers may be responsible for return shipping depending on the reason for the return. Return instructions will be provided once a return request has been approved.
